Common Questions

Where can I register for the events?

You can register online through our registration form at register. The registration deadline is April 8, 2026.

How do I submit my Multimedia presentation or Photography?

Email your submission to scpscitclubofficial@gmail.com with the subject line as specified in the rulebook. Submission deadline is April 10, 2026 before 5:00 PM.

What should I bring to the venue?

Check the specific event rules in our Rulebook. Generally, bring your own mobile phone, headphones, and data. Laptops and projectors are provided for specific events.

Can I participate as an individual in team events?

Yes, for PUBG and Treasure Hunt, individual registrations are accepted and you'll be grouped with other participants.

How will I know if I've qualified for the finals?

Results will be notified via email and announced on our social media channels within 24 hours of the qualifying event.

What if there's a scheduling conflict?

Contact us immediately at scpscitclubofficial@gmail.com with your registration ID and we'll try to accommodate you if possible.
