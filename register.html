Frequently Asked Questions

Can I register for multiple events?

Yes! You can register for as many events as you'd like. Each event has its own rules and schedule.

Is there a registration fee?

Registration details will be provided in the form. Please check the form for fee information and payment methods.

Can I change my events after registering?

You can modify your registered events. Contact us at scpscitclubofficial@gmail.com for assistance.

What if I have registration issues?

If you encounter problems, email us at scpscitclubofficial@gmail.com with your registration details.

Do I need to attend all 3 days?

No. Attend only the days your registered events are scheduled. Check the rulebook for specific dates.

How will I receive event updates?

You'll be added to a WhatsApp group and receive email updates after registration confirmation.
