Submission Format

  • To: scpscitclubofficial@gmail.com
  • Subject: Photography Submission
  • Body:
    • Reg ID:
    • Name:
    • Class:
    • Roll:
  • Attach:
    • Original Photo File
    • Edited Photo File

Theme

"Stories Without Words"

Rules

  • ✅ Photos must be original
  • ✅ Each photo must have a title
  • ✅ Photo must reflect the given theme
  • ✅ Participants must explain their work
  • ❌ No plagiarism allowed

Submission Requirements

  • Both original and edited files must be submitted
  • Original file must contain clear EXIF data

Editing Rules

  • ✅ Only Snapseed & Lightroom allowed
  • ❌ Filters, presets, and LUTs are NOT allowed
  • ✅ Basic spot healing & retouch allowed
  • ❌ Heavy manipulation is NOT allowed

Disqualification

  • Original file not submitted
  • Edited file missing
  • EXIF data removed/edited/fake
  • Filters/presets/LUTs used
  • Heavy manipulation detected
  • AI-generated or AI-enhanced editing used
  • Photo plagiarized or stolen
  • Photo contains watermark, logo, or signature

🗺️ Treasure Hunt

Overview

A team-based clue-solving adventure across campus locations. Navigate through challenges to reach the final treasure!

Team Formation

  • Team Size: 3 members per team
  • Individual participants can join and be grouped

Registration & Gameplay

  • Registration: Separate booth (Must register before start)
  • First Clue: Given after confirmation
  • Clue Flow: Solve clues → move to next location
  • Continue: Until final stage

Final Challenge & Winner

Final puzzle determines the winner!

Winner is the team that:

  • Reaches final stage first
  • Solves the final puzzle correctly

Rules (Critical)

  • ❌ No mobile/electronic devices
  • ❌ No outside help allowed
  • ❌ No cheating or clue sharing between teams
  • ❌ No fighting or unsportsmanlike behavior
  • ✅ Stay within designated area

Assistance & Winning Criteria

  • Hints: Volunteers may give limited hints
  • Criteria: Completion of clues, Accuracy, and Time

Organizer Authority

Organizers reserve the right to:

  • Modify rules as needed
  • Disqualify teams for violations
  • Make final decisions

📌 Subject to rescheduling.

⚽ E-Football (1 vs 1)

Tournament Schedule

  • Online Rounds: April 9, 10
  • Final: April 11

Tournament Format

  • Group Stage: Groups of 4 players
  • Advancement: Only group winner advances
  • Knockout Stage: Group winners → Semifinals/Quarterfinals
  • Final: Winners of semifinals compete

Points System

Result Points
Win3 points
Draw1 point
Loss0 points

Tiebreaker

If teams have equal points:

  • Primary: Goal Difference (GD)

Match Settings

  • Match Time: 7 minutes (basic)
  • Injuries: Off
  • Player Condition: Good

Substitutions & Extra Time

  • Normal Time: 5 substitutions allowed
  • Extra Time: +3 additional substitutions
  • If Draw: Extra Time + Penalties (only after group stage)

Result Submission

Submit result within 5 minutes of match completion

Include:

  • Screenshot of final score
  • Goal scorers list
